## How billing works

Velt is priced on **Monthly Active Documents (MADs)** — documents that have active collaboration in a given month. Just connecting to Velt without active collaboration on a document does not count toward MADs.

For exact MAD allowances, simultaneous connection limits, storage limits, support channels, and add-ons (such as AI Enhancements), refer to the [official pricing page](https://velt.dev/pricing).
